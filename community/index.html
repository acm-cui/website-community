Welcome to the Conversational User Interfaces (CUI) community website. CUI (pronounced "coo-ee") was founded in 2018 to bring together researchers and practitioners from a wide range of disciplines examining the design of speech and language technologies. In late 2022, CUI joined SIGCHI , the Association for Computing Machinery  Special Interest Group on Computer-Human Interaction.

Today, CUI is overseen by a steering committee, who organise the annual CUI conference and a number of satellite workshops] at various conferences across HCI and related disciplines. The underlying purpose of CUI is to promote excellent research in human-centred issues of conversational user interfaces, while also developing a world class community of research and practice around CUIs.

We look forward to meeting you at a future CUI event.
